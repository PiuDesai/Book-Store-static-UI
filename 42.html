THE GIRL ON THE TRAIN

"The Girl on the Train" is a psychological thriller novel written by Paula Hawkins. It follows the story of Rachel Watson, a recently divorced woman who becomes obsessed with observing a couple, Megan and Scott Hipwell, from her daily train commute. When Megan goes missing, Rachel becomes entangled in the investigation and discovers that she may have a connection to the case. The novel unfolds through multiple perspectives, revealing the complex and unreliable nature of memory and perception. As Rachel delves deeper into the mystery, she uncovers dark secrets about herself and those around her. "The Girl on the Train" is a gripping and suspenseful thriller that explores themes of obsession, addiction, and the consequences of past actions
