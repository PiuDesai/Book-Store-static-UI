A Gentleman in Moscow

A Gentleman in Moscow" is a novel by Amor Towles, published in 2016. It follows the story of Count Alexander Rostov, an aristocrat who is sentenced to house arrest in the Metropol Hotel in Moscow during the Russian Revolution. The novel spans several decades and explores themes of resilience, friendship, and the power of literature. It received widespread acclaim for its elegant prose, rich characterization, and evocative depiction of historical events.
