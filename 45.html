THE TURN OF THE KEY

The Turn of the Key" is a thriller novel written by Ruth Ware. It follows the story of a young woman named Rowan Caine who takes a job as a live-in nanny at a remote and luxurious house in the Scottish Highlands. However, soon after starting her job, strange and unsettling events begin to occur, leading Rowan to fear for her safety and the safety of the children in her care. As she tries to unravel the mysteries surrounding the house and its previous occupants, Rowan becomes increasingly entangled in a web of lies, secrets, and betrayal. "The Turn of the Key" is a suspenseful and atmospheric thriller that keeps readers guessing until the very end.
