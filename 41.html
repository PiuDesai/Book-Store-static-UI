THE SLIENT PATIENT

"The Silent Patient" is a psychological thriller novel by Alex Michaelides. It tells the story of Alicia Berenson, a talented painter who is accused of murdering her husband, Gabriel, and then stops speaking. Theo Faber, a forensic psychotherapist, becomes obsessed with Alicia's case and takes on her therapy at the Grove, a secure forensic unit. As Theo delves deeper into Alicia's past and tries to unravel the mystery of why she killed her husband, he uncovers dark secrets and unexpected twists. The novel explores themes of trauma, obsession, and the complexities of the human mind. "The Silent Patient" is a gripping and suspenseful page-turner that keeps readers guessing until the very end.
