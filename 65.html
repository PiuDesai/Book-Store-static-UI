THE MAGIC OF THINKING BIG

"The Magic of Thinking Big" is a self-help book written by David J. Schwartz. Published in 1959, it explores the concept of mindset and how it impacts success and achievement. The book emphasizes the importance of thinking positively, setting high goals, and believing in oneself. Schwartz presents practical strategies for overcoming fear, building confidence, and developing a success-oriented mindset. Through anecdotes, examples, and exercises, "The Magic of Thinking Big" inspires readers to dream big and take action to achieve their goals. It has become a classic in the self-help genre and has helped countless individuals unlock their potential and achieve greater success in both their personal and professional lives.

  • Author: David J. Schwartz
  • Genre:Self-help book
  • Publisher:Simon and Schuster.
