HOMEGOING

"Homegoing" is a novel by Yaa Gyasi, published in 2016. The book follows the descendants of two half-sisters, Effia and Esi, from 18th-century Ghana to present-day America. Each chapter focuses on a different member of the family, exploring their experiences and struggles across generations. "Homegoing" examines themes of identity, heritage, and the lasting impact of slavery and colonization on individuals and families. The novel received critical acclaim for its ambitious scope and powerful storytelling.
