VERITY

"Verity" is a thriller novel written by Colleen Hoover. It tells the story of Lowen Ashleigh, a struggling writer who is offered a lucrative opportunity to complete the remaining books in a successful series by the bestselling author Verity Crawford, who has been incapacitated by an accident. Lowen discovers Verity's hidden manuscript, revealing disturbing truths about Verity and her family. As Lowen becomes increasingly entangled in the lives of the Crawford family, she must confront dark secrets and deception that threaten her safety. "Verity" is a suspenseful and twisted tale of obsession, manipulation, and the blurred lines between truth and fiction.

  • Author: Colleen Hoover.
  • Genre: popular thiller
  • Publisher: Colleen Hoover.
