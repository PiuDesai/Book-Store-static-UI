THE PILLARS OF EARTH

The Pillars of the Earth" is set in 12th-century England and follows the construction of a cathedral in the fictional town of Kingsbridge. The novel intertwines the lives of various characters, including Tom Builder, a master builder; Prior Philip, a monk; Aliena, the daughter of a disgraced nobleman; and William Hamleigh, a ruthless nobleman. The story explores themes of power, politics, love, and betrayal against the backdrop of medieval England. As the cathedral rises, the characters' lives become increasingly intertwined, leading to dramatic twists and turns as they navigate the challenges of their time
  • Originally published: 1989
  • Author: Ken Follet
  • Genre:Historical Novel
  • Publisher:William Morrow.
