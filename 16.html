WAR AND PEACE

"War and Peace" is a classic novel by the Russian author Leo Tolstoy. It was first published in 1869. The novel tells the story of five Russian aristocratic families during the Napoleonic Wars. It explores themes of love, war, politics, and the human condition against the backdrop of historical events. "War and Peace" is renowned for its epic scope, intricate plot, and rich character development.
